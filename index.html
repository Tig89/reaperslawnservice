                    Views

                    • Inbox - Capture everything here first
                    • Today - Committed tasks for today
                    • Tomorrow - Planned for tomorrow (rolls over at midnight)
                    • Next - Ready to do, not scheduled
                    • Waiting - Blocked on someone else
                    • Someday - Maybe later, no commitment

                    ACE Scores (Priority)

                    • A (Impact) - Value created: 1=trivial, 5=life-changing
                    • C (Consequences) - Pain if skipped: 1=none, 5=disaster
                    • E (Friction) - Effort required: 1=easy, 5=exhausting (lowers priority)

                    LMT Bonuses (Do It Now)

                    • L (Leverage) - Unblocks other tasks? 0-2
                    • M (Energy) - Matches current state? 0-2
                    • T (Time) - Must be today? 0-2

                    Top 3 Monster Rule

                    Your Top 3 can have at most 1 "monster" task (90+ min or low confidence). This prevents overcommitting.

                    Gestures
